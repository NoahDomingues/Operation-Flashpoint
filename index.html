Operation Flashpoint: Dragon Rising

Released in 2009, Dragon Rising transports players to the fictional island of Skira, where they experience modern warfare in an intense and highly realistic tactical shooter. Developed by Codemasters, the game challenges players with open-world combat and dynamic squad-based gameplay.

  • Massive open environments with realistic military tactics
  • Advanced ballistics and weapon mechanics
  • Co-op gameplay for coordinated team-based strategies

Operation Flashpoint: Red River

In 2011, Codemasters expanded on their formula with Red River, bringing a new focus on squad-based military realism in Tajikistan. This title emphasizes teamwork, dynamic campaigns, and a gritty narrative that deepens the immersive experience.

  • Highly structured tactical squad gameplay
  • Authentic military storytelling and missions
  • Improved command system for issuing orders

Operation Flashpoint: Cold War Crisis

The legendary **Cold War Crisis** (2001) remains one of the most influential military shooters. Developed by Bohemia Interactive and published by Codemasters, the game delivers an unparalleled sandbox military experience with detailed vehicle mechanics, expansive battlefields, and deep simulation elements.

  • Unmatched freedom in tactical mission approaches
  • Wide range of vehicles, weapons, and AI-driven enemies
  • Groundbreaking realism for its time
